Visit Mežaparks

Enjoy nature, sport, and culture in this green

Top three activities to do in Mežaparks

Explore Mežaparks Great Bandstand

The open-air stage is a venue for the Latvian Song and Dance Festival. You can visit a concert there or just go inside and enjoy architecture.

Enjoy different types of activities

Large urban park is a perfect place for spending time with children, romantic boat trip or
a disc golf game with friends.

Take a quiet architecture walk

Visit a picturesque residential area full of beautiful early 20th century wooden villas.
